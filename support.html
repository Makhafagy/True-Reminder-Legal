Support Policy / Contact

Effective date: January 19, 2026 (Pacific Time) • Last updated: January 19, 2026 (Pacific Time)

Contact: truereminderapp@gmail.com

1) What support includes

  • Bug reports and troubleshooting help.
  • Help understanding features (notifications, Shared Spaces, Copilot, backup/export/import).
  • Privacy requests for backend data we control (for example, a registered push token record).
  • Abuse reports and Shared Space takedown reports (see below).

2) What support does not include

  • Emergency support or time-critical guarantees.
  • Recovering content you deleted from iCloud/CloudKit (Apple controls that storage).
  • Granting refunds for Apple-billed purchases (refunds are handled by Apple).
  • Professional advice (medical, legal, financial, safety).

3) Response times

This is a solo-developer app. Support is provided on a best‑effort basis. I aim to respond within 2 business days, but response time is not guaranteed.

4) What to include in a support request

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • Whether notifications are enabled for True Reminder in iOS Settings

Please avoid sending sensitive personal information in screenshots or logs.

5) Subscriptions, cancellations, refunds

  • Manage/cancel: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Refunds: request refunds through Apple’s refund flow (Apple decides refund eligibility).

6) Shared Space abuse / takedown reports

If you believe content in a Shared Space is abusive or infringes your rights, email truereminderapp@gmail.com with:

  • a description of the issue,
  • any relevant Space name or context you can provide, and
  • screenshots or evidence you are comfortable sharing.

7) Security reports

If you believe you found a security vulnerability, email truereminderapp@gmail.com with details so it can be investigated.
