Acceptable Use Policy

Effective date: January 19, 2026 (Pacific Time) • Last updated: January 19, 2026 (Pacific Time)

This policy helps keep Shared Spaces, Copilot, and related services safe and reliable.

1) Don’t misuse the Service

  • Do not use the Service to violate laws or infringe others’ rights.
  • Do not attempt to gain unauthorized access to systems, accounts, or data.
  • Do not upload, transmit, or distribute malware, exploit code, or harmful content.
  • Do not interfere with the Service’s operation (including denial of service, scraping, or bypassing rate limits or access controls).
  • Do not attempt to bypass subscriptions/entitlements, paywalls, or security protections.

2) Shared Spaces rules

Sharing links are sensitive. Treat Space links like passwords. If you share a link, anyone with the link may be able to access and edit the Space.
  • Only share Spaces with people you trust.
  • Do not share confidential or highly sensitive information in Spaces.
  • No harassment, threats, hate, or abuse toward other members.
  • No impersonation or deceptive activity (for example, pretending to be someone else to gain access).

3) Copilot rules (if used)

  • Do not submit content you do not have the right to share.
  • Do not submit highly sensitive personal information (medical records, financial account numbers, authentication codes).
  • Do not use Copilot to generate or facilitate wrongdoing.
  • Copilot output is suggestions only—verify before saving or acting.

4) Enforcement

We may suspend or limit access to optional services (for example, Copilot or push endpoints) if we reasonably believe there is abuse, a security risk, or a legal compliance need. We may also cooperate with law enforcement or respond to valid legal requests where required.

5) Reporting

To report abuse (including Shared Space issues), email truereminderapp@gmail.com with relevant details.

Build simple. Respect privacy.
