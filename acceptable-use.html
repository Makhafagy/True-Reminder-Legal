Acceptable Use Policy

Effective date: January 10, 2026 (Pacific Time) • Last updated: January 10, 2026 (Pacific Time)

This policy helps keep Shared Spaces, Copilot, and backend services safe and reliable.

1) Do not misuse the App

  • Do not use the App to violate laws or infringe others’ rights.
  • Do not attempt to gain unauthorized access to systems, accounts, or data.
  • Do not upload or transmit malware, exploit code, or harmful content.
  • Do not interfere with the App’s operation (e.g., denial of service, abuse of APIs, bypassing rate limits).

2) Shared Spaces rules

  • Only invite people you trust. Members may see and (depending on permissions) edit shared content.
  • Do not share confidential or highly sensitive information in Spaces.
  • Do not harass, threaten, or abuse other members.

3) Copilot rules (if used)

  • Do not submit content you do not have the right to share.
  • Do not submit highly sensitive personal information (medical records, financial account numbers, etc.).
  • Copilot output is suggestions only—verify before saving.

4) Enforcement

We may suspend or limit access to optional services (e.g., Copilot or backend endpoints) if we reasonably believe there is abuse, security risk, or legal compliance need.

Build simple. Respect privacy.
