True Reminder — Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 10, 2026 (Pacific Time) • Last updated: January 10, 2026 (Pacific Time)

Not legal advice. This policy describes how the app works and is intended to be transparent. If you have questions, contact us.

True Reminder (the “App”) is developed and operated by Mahmoud Khafagy (an individual) (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes and how it’s used.

Summary: Reminders and Shared Spaces sync using Apple iCloud/CloudKit. The App can use Apple Push Notification service (APNs) (including silent/background pushes) to deliver notifications and help devices refresh. If you use Copilot, your request text is sent to our backend and to OpenAI (GPT-5 mini) to generate suggestions. We don’t sell personal information and we don’t run third-party ads.

1) Information we process

  • iCloud / CloudKit reminder data (core app). Reminder content (e.g., titles, schedules, recurrence, notes) and Shared Space metadata are stored in your Apple iCloud account using CloudKit. Shared Spaces are visible only to members you invite or who join via an Apple share.
  • Push notification data (APNs). The App receives a device token from Apple (APNs). If you enable push-assisted refresh, the App may send the device token to our backend so we can: (a) register the token, and (b) send notification requests via Apple APNs (including silent pushes). We may store: device token, environment (sandbox/production), app bundle identifier, and optional internal user identifier (if provided).
  • App settings (on-device). Preferences such as UI settings and notification configuration are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchases. Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive basic entitlement status (e.g., whether Pro is active). We do not receive your payment card details.
  • Optional: Copilot. If you use Copilot, we process the text you submit and may process limited context you choose to include (e.g., reminder titles) to generate suggested actions. Copilot requests are sent to our backend and to OpenAI (GPT-5 mini) for processing.
  • Diagnostics & security logs (limited). We may process limited technical logs to operate and secure the service (e.g., request timing, error codes, rate limiting, and abuse prevention). We do not intend to log your reminder content for diagnostics.

2) How we use information

  • To provide reminders and notifications, including scheduling and sync across your devices via iCloud.
  • To support Shared Spaces collaboration and membership changes.
  • To provide Copilot suggestions (if used).
  • To operate, secure, and debug the App and backend services (including push token registration and push delivery).
  • To validate purchases and unlock Pro features based on Apple entitlement status.

3) Where your data lives & who can access it

  • Reminders & Spaces: stored in your iCloud/CloudKit. Shared Space members can access content in that Space per permissions.
  • Push token backend: if enabled, device tokens may be stored on our backend (hosted on Render) for push delivery and refresh requests.
  • No public database: we do not provide a public directory, public profiles, or a public feed of reminders/spaces.

4) Sharing & third parties

  • Apple: iCloud/CloudKit (sync & collaboration), APNs (notifications), App Store (purchases).
  • Hosting: backend infrastructure may be hosted on Render (and its underlying infrastructure providers).
  • Copilot: Copilot requests are sent to OpenAI for processing and response generation.
  • No selling: we do not sell personal information and do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5) Legal bases (EEA/UK)

If you are in the EEA/UK/Switzerland, we process information to provide the App (contract), to operate and secure services (legitimate interests), and with your consent where required (e.g., certain optional features).

6) Retention

  • iCloud data: you control retention by managing reminders/spaces and iCloud settings.
  • Push tokens: retained while needed for push delivery, then deleted or invalidated when replaced/unregistered, or upon request.
  • Operational logs: retained only as long as reasonably necessary for reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

7) Your choices

  • Notifications: you can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • iCloud: you can disable iCloud for the App; sync and Shared Spaces will stop working.
  • Copilot: Copilot is optional; you can avoid using it.
  • Delete data: delete reminders/spaces inside the App (CloudKit). For backend-stored push tokens, contact truereminderapp@gmail.com.

8) Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact truereminderapp@gmail.com.

If Apple’s App Store age rating permits younger users in your region, we still do not intend to collect personal information from children under 13.

9) Security

We use reasonable safeguards, including HTTPS. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10) California privacy notice

We do not sell personal information and do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To request access/deletion of backend-stored data we control (e.g., push tokens), contact us. iCloud/CloudKit content is controlled through your Apple account and the App.

11) Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll update the “Last updated” date and may provide additional notice where appropriate.

12) Contact

Privacy questions: truereminderapp@gmail.com
