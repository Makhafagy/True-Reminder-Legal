True Reminder — Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 28, 2025

True Reminder (the “App”) is developed and operated by Mahmoud Khafagy (an individual) (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what information the App processes and how it’s used.

Summary: The App stores reminders and shared “Spaces” in your iCloud/CloudKit. There is no public feed or public database. If you use the optional Copilot feature, your request is sent to our backend (AWS) and to an AI provider to generate suggested actions. We do not sell your data and we don’t run ads.

1) Information we process

  • iCloud / CloudKit reminder data (core app). Reminder titles, schedules, status, and shared “Space” metadata are stored in your iCloud account using Apple CloudKit. Shared Spaces are only visible to the members you invite or who join via your share.
  • App settings (on-device). Preferences such as UI settings, sounds, and notification configuration are stored locally on your device (and may be included in your device backups depending on your iOS settings).
  • Purchases. Subscriptions and in‑app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive basic entitlement status (for example, whether Pro is active). We do not receive your payment card details.
  • Optional: Copilot / planning requests. If you use Copilot, we process what you type (your prompt) and may also send contextual text you choose to include (for example: Space names and reminder titles) so Copilot can generate suggested reminder actions. Copilot also sends technical context like your timezone and app version so results are scheduled correctly.
  • Diagnostics & security logs (limited). Apple may provide crash/performance diagnostics if you opt in at the OS level. For Copilot requests, our backend may log limited technical metadata (for example request timing, app version, and usage metrics) to operate the service, prevent abuse, and debug reliability.

2) How we use information

  • To provide reminders: schedule local notifications, show upcoming items, and sync across your devices via iCloud.
  • To support shared Spaces: let members collaborate on reminders inside a private shared Space.
  • To provide Copilot (if used): generate suggested reminder actions from your request.
  • To operate and secure the app and Copilot: monitor reliability, prevent abuse, and fix bugs.
  • To validate purchases: unlock Pro features when Apple indicates you’re entitled.

3) Where your data lives & who can access it

  • Reminders & Spaces: stored in your iCloud/CloudKit. In shared Spaces, members you invite can view (and depending on permissions, edit) items in that Space.
  • No public database: the App does not provide a public directory, public profiles, or a public feed of reminders/spaces.
  • Developer access: we do not operate our own database for your iCloud reminders/spaces. Your iCloud content is managed under your Apple ID and Apple’s iCloud security controls.
  • Copilot requests (optional): Copilot requests are processed by our backend service (hosted on AWS) and sent to an AI provider to generate output. This is separate from iCloud sync.

4) Sharing & third parties

  • With people you invite: shared Space content is visible to Space members.
  • With Apple: iCloud/CloudKit is required for sync & collaboration; the App Store is used for purchases.
  • With service providers (Copilot only): Copilot requests are routed through our hosting and AI providers to generate results.
  • No advertising: we do not sell your personal information and do not share it with ad networks for targeted advertising.

5) Your choices

  • Don’t use Copilot: Copilot is optional. You can use the app as a normal reminders + shared Spaces app without it.
  • Be careful with sensitive data: avoid entering highly sensitive personal information into reminder titles or Copilot prompts.
  • Manage iCloud: you can disable iCloud for the App in iOS settings (sync/collaboration will stop).
  • Shared Spaces: you can leave Spaces, remove members (if you have permissions), or delete content you created.

6) Retention

  • iCloud data: you control retention by managing reminders/spaces and iCloud settings.
  • Copilot logs: limited operational logs may be retained for a period needed for reliability and security.

7) Security

We rely on Apple iCloud security plus your device security (Face ID/Touch ID/passcode). For Copilot, we use standard transport encryption (HTTPS) between the app and backend providers.

8) Children

The App isn’t directed to children and we don’t knowingly collect personal information from children.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We’ll update the effective date above and may provide additional notice in the app or App Store listing where appropriate.

10) Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email: truereminderapp@gmail.com

11) California residents’ rights

If you’re a California resident, you may have rights under the CCPA/CPRA. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise applicable rights, contact us at the email above.
