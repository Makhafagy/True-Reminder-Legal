True Reminder — Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 19, 2026 (Pacific Time) • Last updated: January 19, 2026 (Pacific Time)

True Reminder (the “App”) is developed and operated by Mahmoud Khafagy (an individual) (“we,” “us,” “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the App and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

Plain-language summary: Your reminders and Shared Spaces are stored in Apple iCloud/CloudKit. The App may register an APNs device token with our push service to help deliver pushes (including silent/background pushes). If you use Copilot, your request text is sent to our backend and to OpenAI to generate suggestions. We do not sell personal information and we do not run third‑party ads.

1) Information we process

  • Reminder content and Shared Spaces (stored in Apple iCloud/CloudKit). This includes reminder titles, schedules, recurrence settings, notes, and Shared Space metadata. Shared Spaces are collaborative: members who have access to a Space can view and edit the Space’s content.
  • Sharing links and collaboration. If you create or join a Shared Space, the share link and membership actions are handled through Apple’s CloudKit sharing mechanisms. Treat share links as sensitive: anyone with the link may be able to access the shared content.
  • iCloud backup and restore for local reminders (optional). If you enable “Back up to iCloud,” the App stores a backup for certain local reminders so you can restore after reinstall. Availability depends on your iCloud storage, device storage, and Apple services.
  • Export/import backup files (optional). The App can export a backup file (for example, a “.trbackup” file) and import backups. Where the file is stored depends on where you save it (device storage, iCloud Drive, Files, etc.). We do not receive exported files unless you choose to share them with us for support.
  • Push notification data (APNs token) and push service records. Apple provides the App with a device token for push notifications. If push-assisted refresh is enabled, the App may send that token to our push backend so we can register it and request pushes via Apple APNs. The push service may store token metadata such as: token, environment (sandbox/production), bundle identifier, user identifier (CloudKit record name), install identifier, platform, validity status, and timestamps.
  • Copilot input (optional). If you use Copilot, we process the text you submit (and any context you choose to include) to generate suggestions. Copilot requests are sent to our backend and to OpenAI for processing.
  • Diagnostics, security, and operational logs. We and our hosting providers may process limited logs needed to operate and protect the Service (for example: request IDs, error codes, rate limiting, and basic request metadata). Copilot backend logs are designed to avoid storing full prompts by default and may instead log limited metadata (such as request timing and privacy-preserving hashes/lengths) for debugging and abuse prevention.
  • Purchases and entitlements. Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We may receive entitlement status (for example, whether Pro features are active). We do not receive your full payment card details.
  • Support communications. If you email us or contact us through the App, we process the information you provide (including attachments you choose to send).

2) How we use information

  • To provide reminders, scheduling, and notifications.
  • To sync and collaborate through iCloud/CloudKit and Shared Spaces.
  • To register device tokens and request pushes via Apple APNs (including silent/background pushes) to improve cross-device updates.
  • To provide Copilot suggestions if you choose to use Copilot.
  • To operate, secure, debug, and prevent abuse of the Service (including rate limiting).
  • To validate purchases and unlock Pro features based on Apple entitlement status.

3) Sharing and third parties

  • Apple: iCloud/CloudKit (storage, sync, sharing), APNs (push notifications), App Store (billing).
  • OpenAI: processes Copilot requests to generate suggestions.
  • Hosting providers: we use infrastructure providers to run backend services (for example, an AWS-hosted Copilot backend and a Render-hosted push backend), which may process limited operational logs as part of providing hosting and security services.
  • No sale / no ad targeting: we do not sell personal information and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

4) Retention

  • iCloud/CloudKit content: you control retention by deleting reminders/spaces in the App and managing iCloud settings.
  • Push token records: retained while needed for push delivery and reliability (for example, until replaced, invalidated, or deleted upon request).
  • Copilot backend logs: retained for a limited period for reliability and security (for example, a 7-day CloudWatch retention setting may apply to that backend’s logs).
  • Support communications: retained as long as needed to respond and maintain records for safety and abuse prevention.

5) Your choices and controls

  • Notifications: manage permissions in iOS Settings.
  • iCloud: you can disable iCloud for the App; sync and Shared Spaces will not work if iCloud/CloudKit is unavailable.
  • Copilot: Copilot is optional; you can avoid using it.
  • Delete content: delete reminders/spaces inside the App (CloudKit). You can also manage iCloud storage in iOS settings.
  • Request deletion of backend data we control: email truereminderapp@gmail.com (for example, to request deletion of a registered push token record).

6) Legal bases (EEA/UK/Switzerland)

Where applicable, we process information to provide the Service (contract), to operate and secure the Service (legitimate interests), and with your consent where required (for example, optional features and permissions).

7) Security

We use reasonable safeguards such as HTTPS in transit. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8) Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child provided personal information to us, contact truereminderapp@gmail.com.

9) Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll update the “Last updated” date and may provide additional notice where appropriate.

10) Contact

Privacy questions: truereminderapp@gmail.com
