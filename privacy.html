True Reminder — Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 23, 2025

True Reminder (“the App”) is developed and operated by Mahmoud Khafagy (“we”, “us”). This policy explains how the App handles information. It’s designed to be short, clear, and concise.

Summary: We use your iCloud account to store and sync your reminders and shared spaces. We don’t sell data, we don’t use third-party ads, and payment is processed by Apple.

1) What we collect

  • iCloud / CloudKit data. Reminder titles, times, schedules, shared “Spaces,” and related metadata are saved in your iCloud account to enable syncing and collaboration.
  • App settings. Local preferences (e.g., sound selection, repeat options) are stored on your device.
  • Purchases. Subscription and one-time purchase status is handled by Apple’s App Store. We receive a basic status flag to unlock Pro features; we never receive your card details.
  • Diagnostics (optional). Apple may provide anonymized crash/performance reports so we can fix bugs and improve reliability.

2) How we use data

  • Provide core functionality: schedule reminders, send notifications, and sync across devices via iCloud.
  • Enable collaboration in shared Spaces that you join or create.
  • Validate purchases and restore access to Pro features.
  • Improve stability via crash/performance diagnostics.

3) Sharing

  • With people you invite. If you choose to share a Space, members can view the reminders within that Space.
  • With Apple services. iCloud, App Store, and TestFlight are required to sync data and manage purchases.
  • No ads, no brokers. We do not sell data or share it with advertising networks.

4) Storage & security

  • Reminder data is stored in your iCloud containers and is protected by Apple’s security controls and your Apple ID.
  • We do not have direct access to your iCloud content.
  • On-device data remains on your device unless you back it up with Apple services.

5) Retention & deletion

  • You control retention through iCloud. Deleting a reminder or Space will remove it from your devices and iCloud, subject to Apple’s normal sync and backup behavior.
  • Crash logs provided by Apple may be retained in App Store Connect for a limited time for debugging.

6) Your choices

  • Leave or remove members from a shared Space at any time.
  • Delete reminders/Spaces you created.
  • Disable iCloud for the app in iOS Settings → your Apple ID → iCloud if you prefer local-only use (sync/collaboration will stop).
  • Request support or ask privacy questions at truereminderapp@gmail.com

7) Children

The app is rated 4+ and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective date" at the top of the policy. Your continued use of the App after any such changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

9) Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: truereminderapp@gmail.com

10) California Residents’ Rights

If you are a resident of California, you have rights under California law (including the CCPA and CPRA). These include:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, or share about you.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information (subject to certain exceptions).
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information.
  • The right not to be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at truereminderapp@gmail.com.

11) Acceptance

By using True Reminder, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

12) Governing law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of California and applicable U.S. federal law, without regard to conflicts of law principles.

13) Security & your responsibilities

We rely on Apple’s iCloud security and your device security (passcode, Face ID/Touch ID). We do not control or store your iCloud password. You are responsible for keeping your Apple ID, device, and backups secure. We are not liable for loss or unauthorized access resulting from issues with your Apple ID, device compromise, or iCloud service disruptions.
